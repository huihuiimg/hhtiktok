Privacy Policy

This tool hhtiktok is a third-party TikTok content management platform authorized via TikTok Open API.

1. Data We Collect

We only obtain basic public profile information and video operation permissions authorized by users through TikTok OAuth login. We will not collect sensitive personal information without explicit user consent.

2. Use of Data

All obtained data is only used for core functions: video upload, content data statistics and multi-account management. We will never sell, rent or share user data with any third-party commercial institutions.

3. Data Storage & Security

User authorized tokens and content data are stored in encrypted form. We adopt industry-standard encryption measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure or tampering of data.

4. User Rights

Users can revoke TikTok authorization at any time in TikTok official platform, and we will clear all your relevant stored data after revocation.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact our developer team.
